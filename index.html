  • A Letter for You

for srasta

hi srasta,

i don’t really know where to start. maybe i just missed you a little more today than usual. maybe i just wanted to put a small piece of how i feel somewhere that feels real.

it’s been two years. wild, right? time keeps moving.

you still come up in the quiet moments. not loudly. not painfully. just... gently. aba maybe when i am happy or when i accomplish something and i dont have you to share it with, or when someone laughs in a way that sounds like you used to...

i don’t want anything. i’m not trying to change the story. i just wanted to say: you really mattered to me. deeply. beautifully. and a huge part of me has been always carrying you around regardless of if im very happy or sad
deep down, i still read your letters...

i hope you’re okay. i hope you’re laughing often. and i hope you still walk like the world’s your own little movie...

every time i discover new songs on spotify, i remember how much i loved sharing them with you.
every time i think of kathmandu and miss you, i make spinach and feta ravioli and listen to prateek kuhad (seriously—can you believe it? prateek kuhad and me haha).
it's funny to me how now i’m a crazy matcha drinker even though we never drank it together. somehow, when i do, it feels like i’m drinking it with you.
every time i buy something and mess up the size, i still think of how thusking you would be when i got your shoe size wrong.
every time i’ve failed, i think of how you believed in me. the more i grow, the more i realize how proud i am of the person you are...
you can accomplish all your goals srasta, you are genuinely the kindest person i have ever welcomed in my life, and in the end as we live, this is what makes me respect you even more
and every time i’m at my lowest, i still ask myself—where did potey come from?

this little corner of the internet is just my way of saying: thank you. for being you. for being part of my story.

– shashwat
